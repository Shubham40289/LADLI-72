Our Story

About LADLI 72

Real Solutions For Today's Women

Who We Are

Born in Hisar,
Dressed Across Haryana

LADLI 72 started with a simple belief — every woman deserves to walk with confidence and style, without compromising on comfort or budget. Located in the heart of Rajguru Market, Hisar, we've built a reputation for offering the most curated collection of women's footwear in the region.

From elegant heels for weddings to everyday sandals and stylish juttis, our collection is designed to match every mood, occasion, and personality. With over 500+ styles available at any time, we're more than just a shoe store — we're your personal style destination.

Follow us on Instagram @ladli.72 for daily new arrivals and inspiration. We also take WhatsApp orders with same-day delivery in Hisar!
